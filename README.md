# Alethia

We are in a complex environment to do business. They must work within a host of constraints - federal, state and local, spanning from employment, to environmental protection, to everything in between. They must also adapt to differing political and consumer sentiment, as well as the unique logistical challenges of each region. All of these disproportionately impact small businesses, as big firms have the capital/resources to analyze these data points to make data driven decisions.

We strive to change that by giving small business and startup builders the same kind of expertise large companies employ teams of general counsels and analysts to achieve. We are here to democratize business intelligence.
